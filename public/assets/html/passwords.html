Password Policy

Secure your accounts with strong passwords

Why a Strong Password is Important

Using a strong password is essential to protect your personal information and online accounts from unauthorized access.

Password Requirements

Your password must meet the following criteria:

  • At least 8 characters long
  • Include at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
  • Include at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
  • Include at least one digit (0-9)
  • Allowed symbols: @, #, _

Tips for Creating a Strong Password

Consider the following tips to enhance your password security:

  • Avoid using easily guessable information (e.g., birthdays, names)
  • Use a mix of different character types
  • Consider using a passphrase for better memorability
  • Change your password regularly and do not reuse passwords across different accounts
